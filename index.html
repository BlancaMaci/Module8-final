Spaghetti Bolognese Recipe

About Recipe Videos

About Me

Hi, I'm Blanca Macias! I love cooking hearty, comforting dishes that bring people together. Spaghetti Bolognese is my favorite because its rich, savory flavors and simple ingredients make it perfect for family gatherings. I enjoy adding a personal twist with a hint of fresh herbs to elevate the dish.

Recipe

Ingredients

  • 400g spaghetti
  • 500g ground beef
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 carrot, finely chopped
  • 1 celery stick, finely chopped
  • 400g canned diced tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 1 cup beef broth
  • 1 tsp dried oregano
  • 1 tsp fresh basil, chopped
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 50g Parmesan cheese, grated

Steps

Step 1: Heat a large pan over medium heat. Add ground beef and cook until browned, about 5-7 minutes. Drain excess fat.

Step 2: In the same pan, add onion, garlic, carrot, and celery. Cook until softened, about 5 minutes.

Step 3: Stir in tomato paste, diced tomatoes, beef broth, oregano, and basil. Simmer for 20-30 minutes, stirring occasionally. Season with salt and pepper.

Step 4: Boil spaghetti in a large pot of salted water until al dente, about 8-10 minutes. Drain and set aside.

Step 5: Combine spaghetti with the sauce. Serve hot, garnished with Parmesan cheese.
